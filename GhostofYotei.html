Ghost of Yotei

Ghost of Yotei is a third person action game that comes as a sequel to the much-acclaimed Ghost of Tsushima. I love this game because the gameplay is some of the best and makes you feel like an actual samurai. The combat was an overall improvement over the last game, giving much more combat option. The story is good but unfortunately, I feel the story in the first game was more impactful. The graphics were also a huge standout, boasting possibly the most realistic of the year. This game came out in 2025 and was probably the best AAA game that came out the whole year, with the only competition being Donkey Kong bonanza. Unfortunately, it did not receive a game of the year nomination because 2025 was a very stacked year for Indie and AA games.
