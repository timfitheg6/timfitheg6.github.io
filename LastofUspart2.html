Last of Us part 2

The Last of us part 2 is a very controversial game. People either loved the themes and direction the game went in or hated it because they believed it disrespected them. For me, I loved the game. I will try not to spoil the game, but the lessons that the creators were trying to portray hit hard for me. Since TLOU is generally known for the story aspects, the main focus of quality lies there. I do understand the complaints or plot holes levied against the game, but I feel that I agree with the themes presented and understood why certain things needed to happen the way they did. On the aspects of gameplay and graphics, I hear few complaints there. It was generally an improvement over the first game with its stealth combat and realistic look. Overall, I understand why it won game of the year in 2020 but also do not dismiss its controversial parts.
