Escape From Duckov

Escape from Duckov is a hilarious little duck game that is deceivingly deep and difficult to play. The main premise is like another game with a similar name “Escape from Tarkov”. The goal of the game is to enter an arena type area collect loot kill enemies and extract from the area safety. The area has various loots such as guns and crafting material all about which is used to upgrade your duck. With each level upgrade to your guns and duck the deeper you can explore.
